# EyeBreak — Product Hunt Launch Assets

## Tagline (60 chars max)
Your eyes deserve a break. Smart 20-20-20 reminders for Mac.

## Short Description (260 chars max)
EyeBreak lives in your menu bar and reminds you to rest your eyes every 20 minutes using the 20-20-20 rule. When it's break time, your screen fills with TV static — no countdown, no distractions, just a boring screen that makes you look away naturally.

## Full Description
### The Problem
We stare at screens for hours without blinking. Notifications? Too easy to dismiss. Timers? You just ignore them. Your eyes need something harder to ignore.

### The Solution
EyeBreak takes a different approach: when it's time for a break, your entire screen fills with TV static noise — like an old broken TV. There's no countdown to stare at, no pretty animation to watch. Just static that naturally makes you look away.

After 20 seconds, it disappears and you're back to work. Simple.

### Features
- **20-20-20 Rule**: Every 20 minutes, look 20 feet away for 20 seconds
- **TV Static Overlay**: Anti-attention design — makes you look away, not at the screen
- **Menu Bar App**: Lives in your menu bar, never in your way
- **Lightweight**: ~100KB, no dependencies, instant launch
- **Bilingual**: Arabic + English, auto-detects system language
- **Free**: Core features are completely free

### Built With
- SwiftUI + AppKit
- No external dependencies
- Built with Claude Code

## Maker Comment
Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I built EyeBreak because I kept forgetting to rest my eyes while coding. Every reminder app I tried was too easy to dismiss — a notification pops up, I swipe it away, back to staring.

So I thought: what if the reminder was so boring you HAD to look away? That's how the TV static idea was born. When break time comes, your screen turns into a broken TV. No countdown (that would make you stare!), no beautiful graphics — just ugly static that makes your eyes naturally want to look somewhere else.

It's tiny (~100KB), lives in your menu bar, and speaks Arabic + English.

Would love your feedback! 🙏
