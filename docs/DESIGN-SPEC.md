## Key Messaging (for any context)

**One-liner**: Your eyes deserve a break. Smart 20-20-20 reminders for Mac.

**Elevator pitch**: EyeBreak reminds you to rest your eyes every 20 minutes. Instead of notifications you'll ignore, it fills your screen with TV static — so boring you naturally look away. 20 seconds later, you're back to work.

**Differentiator**: Anti-attention design. Other apps show you pretty countdowns. We show you ugly static. That's the point — you're not supposed to look at it.
